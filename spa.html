Our Services

At Ainbo Salon & Spa, New York, our services incorporate a holistic approach to wellness. Re-center yourself, soothe your mind, body, and spirit by gifting yourself time to discover the benefits of pampering.
We pride ourselves on creating revitalizing moments for our guests that help reveal inner and outer beauty.
Walk out feeling like a confident you.

Ainbo Salon & Spa offers a soothing and tranquil experience designed to indulge in self-care and self-love:

  1. Rejuvenate your body and mind through relaxation and meditation techniques.
  2. Boost your self-esteem and self-confidence.
  3. Reveal inner and outer beauty through natural and soothing treatments.

Massages

Our comprehensive and holistic approach to wellness and well-being starts with pampering you to release peaceful endorphins. Experience unparalleled indulgence massages that are designed to revitalize, rejuvenate, and refresh in a soothing atmosphere. All body massages and treatments start with a sensory and decadent tension-relieving massage to increase relaxation and promote better sleeping patterns and energy flow.

Hair

At Ainbo Salon & Spa, we believe that healthy, beautiful hair is within reach. Our expert stylists use the latest techniques and high-quality products to rejuvenate and revitalize your locks. From personalized haircuts and styling to deep conditioning treatments and scalp massages, we tailor our services to meet your unique needs. Experience the difference of professional hair care at Ainbo.

All treatments and body massages commence with a sensory and decadent tension-relieving massage, allowing guests to relax, be in the moment, promote better sleep patterns and improve energy flow.

Nails

Our skilled manicurists in NewYork, offer a wide range of treatments to pamper your hands and feet. From classic manicures and pedicures to nail extensions and gel polish, we ensure your nails look and feel their best. Experience the ultimate in nail artistry and leave feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

Facials

Using potent and quality skincare products, we aim to make skin feel healthy and beautiful. Ainbo Salon & Spa, New York, has evidence-based techniques that are personalized to cater to each client. We have the ultimate facials to promote natural collagen flow and elasticity from signature European facials, Hydra facials, Micro-needling, Microdermabrasion, and Light Stim.
